About Crazy Asian Poker

Crazy Asian Poker (CAP) is played extensively in Singapore home games, and is spreading throughout the region with games now in Hong Kong and Sydney. Games often run multiple nights a week.
Games range from $1/$2 to $50/$100. The rules are designed for action, and games play big.
Player numbers are usually capped at seven. This allows players to be dealt six cards each for a double board game, or five cards each for a triple board game.

Your correspondent is a regular in a $1/$2 Singapore home game.
I am the author of the Crazy Asian Poker Game Guide, and contributions to game innovation include Countdown, Pandemic and the Dynamic Points System.
